The Reframe

Three tax and compliance levers, stacked on the same learner

None of these require a SETA discretionary grant to be approved. A grant, if it comes through, is upside on a case that already works without it.

Section 12H

Direct annual and completion tax allowance for every learner on a registered learnership.

ETI Absorption

Monthly PAYE relief for absorbing learners aged 18–29 into permanent roles once qualified.

B-BBEE Scorecard

Skills Development and Employment Equity points that protect tender and preferred-supplier status.

TETA Pipeline

Qualified, tested staff for high-turnover roles — not another agency placement.
